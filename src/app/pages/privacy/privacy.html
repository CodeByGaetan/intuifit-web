Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026
01

Data controller

IntuiFit (the iOS app and this website) is developed and operated by Gaétan La Selve, an independent developer established in France. For any question regarding this policy or your data, contact codebygaetan@gmail.com.

02

Data we collect

IntuiFit records the workout data you enter in the app: workout sessions, sets, and exercises. This data is stored locally on your device only, using Apple's on-device persistence framework (SwiftData), and remains there until you delete it or uninstall the app. It is never transmitted to the developer.

There is no account, no registration, no tracking, no analytics, and no ads. The app does not collect personal information, device identifiers, or usage statistics.

Where processing falls under the GDPR, its legal basis is the performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): providing the workout-tracking service you requested.

03

iCloud sync (Premium)

If you subscribe to Premium, your workout data is synced across your devices via Apple CloudKit, in your private iCloud database. This data is tied to your Apple ID, encrypted by Apple, and not accessible to the developer under any circumstances.

Apple may store iCloud data on servers located outside the European Union, including in the United States. These transfers are governed by Apple's own safeguards, including the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework and standard contractual clauses. See Apple's privacy policy for details.

04

Premium subscription & payments

The Premium subscription is purchased and billed exclusively through Apple's App Store. Payment is processed by Apple: the developer never receives your payment details (card number, billing address, or identity). Apple only provides the developer with anonymized, aggregated transaction reports needed to operate the service.

05

Apple Health

If — and only if — you grant permission, IntuiFit writes workout summaries to Apple Health. The app never reads any data from Apple Health, and Health data is never transmitted to the developer or any third party.

06

Third parties

No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties. IntuiFit contains no third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, or analytics services.

07

Support emails

If you contact support, we process your email address and the content of your message for the sole purpose of answering you (legal basis: our legitimate interest in providing support, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR). Support emails are kept only as long as needed to resolve your request, and no longer than two years, then deleted.

08

This website

This website is edited by Gaétan La Selve as a private individual, and hosted on GitHub Pages, operated by GitHub, Inc., 88 Colin P. Kelly Jr Street, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA. Like any web host, GitHub may log visitors' IP addresses for security and operational purposes; see GitHub's privacy statement.

The website itself sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and loads no third-party resources — fonts are self-hosted, so no request is sent to font providers.

09

Data deletion

Deleting the app removes all local data from your device. If you used iCloud sync, you can delete the synced data at any time via iOS Settings → iCloud → Manage Storage.

10

Your rights

Because the developer is established in France, the GDPR applies to all users, wherever you are located. You may exercise at any time your rights of access, rectification, erasure, portability, restriction of processing, and objection by emailing codebygaetan@gmail.com. Note that since all workout data lives on your device or in your private iCloud, you already hold direct control over it.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in France, the CNIL (cnil.fr) — or with the authority of your country of residence.

11

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app or in applicable law. The current version is always available at this address, with its "last updated" date shown above. Material changes will be highlighted in the app's release notes.
