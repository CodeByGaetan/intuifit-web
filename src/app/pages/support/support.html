FAQ

01How do I log a set?+

Tap an exercise on the Home screen, adjust weight and reps with the steppers, tap "Validate set".

02Where is my data stored?+

Locally on your device. iCloud sync is available with Premium.

03How do I cancel Premium?+

Go to iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → IntuiFit.

04I found a bug, how do I report it?+

Email codebygaetan@gmail.com with your iOS version and a description of the issue.
